Working Title: "Bookmetrix Alert"

Description
Email is sent to the all authors of a book that was published 3 month before to inform about different statistics for the book (bookmetrix) and to activate the author to check his or her personal overview page with features like Book Tracking, Book download, ABPR download or Bookmetrix stats.
Target Groups
Existing Book Author
Goal & Main Call-two-actions
» Inform author about the various book statistics
» Activate author about to check personal "My Publication" page
» Inform author to which ebook collection the new book was assigned to
