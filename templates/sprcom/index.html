Springer Email Redesign

Description
Emails are essential for a lot of target group services, but are also used for marketing emails or for transactional communication.
Since there are more than 50 different email types it's a big project to redesign all of them as each email has different requirements. The following guidelines should help in the process of redesign.
Guidelines
  • Responsive design
  • Focused Texts
  • Clear CTAs
  • Good documentation
  • Golive in batches (bookauthor emails, journal author email, instructor email,...)
